Welcome!

Welcome! I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Applied Mathematics at the University of Washington. I hold an Honours Bachelor of Science from the University of Toronto, where I graduated with distinction in Mathematics and Applied Statistics. I am interested in areas of quantitative research, machine learning, and statistical research. I am still learning and exploring new areas and fields. You can find my work on this page.

Background

I've always enjoyed mathematics, but I never imagined it would become the focus of both my bachelor's and master's degrees. My journey at the University of Toronto started more as an exploration of my interests.

My direction in mathematics was completely transformed when I came to the University of Toronto. Everything changed when I had the opportunity to take courses under renowned mathematicians like Professors Arul Shankar, Myrto Mavraki, and KE Zhang. Learning from them wasn't just about the coursework, it ignited a genuine passion by showing me the creativity and depth of the field.

During my undergrad, I completed an independent study on algebraic numbers with Professor Marina Tvalvazade and took graduate-level courses in neuroscience and machine learning.
